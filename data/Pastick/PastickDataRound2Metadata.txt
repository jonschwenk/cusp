WesternAKSitePHoriz (n=15,152): 
This is an NRCS soil pedon dataset and you’ll need to query a couple of columns to get the data you need for your modelling. 
Layers of dry or ice rich permafrost are designated by the horizon (hzname) suffix of ff and f, respectively (e.g., Cf, Cgjjf, Oaf). 
You’ll need to use that and the horizon top depth (hzdept) to identify those pedons with frozen soil presence/absence in the desired depth range. 
In total there are 305 sites that contain permafrost in the first meter of the ground surface.
